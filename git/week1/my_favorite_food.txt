How to Cook Spaghetti Carbonara

Start by boiling a pot of salted water and cooking the spaghetti until al dente. While the pasta cooks, crisp up diced pancetta in a skillet over medium heat, letting its fat render and infuse with flavor.

In a bowl, whisk together eggs, grated Parmesan, Pecorino Romano, and a generous crack of black pepper to create the sauce.

Once the spaghetti is ready, drain it while reserving some pasta water. Toss the hot pasta into the skillet with the pancetta, coating it in the rendered fat. Turn off the heat, pour in the egg mixture, and toss quickly, using the pasta’s heat to create a creamy sauce. Add a splash of pasta water if needed to adjust the texture.

Serve immediately with extra cheese and black pepper on top for the perfect, comforting dish.
