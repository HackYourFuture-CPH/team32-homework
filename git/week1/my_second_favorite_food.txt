How to Make Margherita Pizza

Start by preparing a simple pizza dough or using a pre-made one. Roll it out into a thin circle and place it on a baking tray or pizza stone. Preheat your oven to the highest temperature, typically 475°F (250°C), for a crisp crust.

Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges. Add slices of fresh mozzarella evenly across the surface, and scatter a few fresh basil leaves for aroma and flavor. Drizzle with a little olive oil for extra richness.

Bake the pizza in the hot oven for 7–10 minutes, or until the crust is golden and crisp, and the cheese is bubbling and slightly browned.

Once out of the oven, let it cool for a moment, then slice and enjoy the perfect balance of a crispy crust, tangy tomato sauce, creamy mozzarella, and fragrant basil. Simple, fresh, and utterly delicious!
